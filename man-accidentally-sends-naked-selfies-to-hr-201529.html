Man Accidentally Sends Naked Selfies to HR

Publish date: 2024-01-31

Extracurricular activities indeed. A 23-year-old man in Illinois accidentally sent naked selfies to a human resources manager who works for a company he was applying for a job with.

 

According to The Chicago Tribune, the company offered a position to the man, who then sent the female HR employee two naked photos of himself via text. The HR manager then informed the police.

The next day, the applicant followed up with the HR manager regarding his offer and, according to Elmhurst police, the HR manager realized that the man’s phone number was associated with the nude images. Not surprisingly, the company retracted the offer of employment.
