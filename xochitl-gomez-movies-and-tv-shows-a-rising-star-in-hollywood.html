Xochitl Gomez Movies and TV Shows: A Rising Star in Hollywood

Publish date: 2024-04-10

Xochitl Gomez is a young and talented actress who has been making waves in the entertainment industry with her impressive performances in various movies and TV shows.

She is best known for playing the role of America Chavez, a superhero who can travel across dimensions, in the Marvel Cinematic Universe film Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness (2022).

She is also known for portraying Dawn Schafer, a socially conscious and outspoken babysitter, in the Netflix series The Baby-Sitters Club (2020).

In this article, we will explore Xochitl Gomez’s age, movies and TV shows, net worth, spouse, children, height, and more.

Xochitl Gomez Age

Xochitl Gomez was born on April 29, 2006, in Los Angeles, California.

She is currently 17 years old as of 2023.

Her name, Xochitl, means “flower” in Nahuatl, an ancient language spoken in Mexico and other Central American countries.

She is of Mexican descent, as her father is from Mexico.

Xochitl Gomez grew up with “a lot of Mexican influences” since her friends and babysitter were Mexican, and her parents spoke Spanish at home.

She has attended public school her entire life.

Xochitl Gomez Height

Xochitl Gomez has a slim build and stands at a height of 5 feet 5 inches or 165 cm.

She weighs around 54 kg or 119 lbs.

She also has distinctive features such as a wide smile, thick eyebrows, and a snub nose.

Xochitl Gomez Movies and TV Shows

Xochitl Gomez began acting at age five, performing in local musical theater productions and student films.

She made her professional debut in 2018 in Raven’s Home, a Disney Channel sitcom.

She also starred in Shadow Wolves (2019), an action movie for which she won a Young Artist Award for Supporting Teen Artist.

In 2020, she appeared in several movies and TV shows, such as Roped, a romantic film; Gentefied, a Netflix comedy-drama series; and The Lone Drone, a short sci-fi film.

However, her breakthrough role came when she joined the cast of The Baby-Sitters Club, an adaptation of Ann M.

Martin’s novel series of the same name, as Dawn Schafer.

Released on Netflix on July 3, 2020, the show garnered critical acclaim.

Entertainment Weekly stated: “Gomez delivers Dawn’s social-justice-themed dialogue with heartfelt frankness.”

In 2022, Xochitl Gomez achieved further recognition and critical praise for her portrayal of America Chavez in the Marvel Cinematic Universe film Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness.

She is the first Latina actress to play a superhero in the MCU.

She also became one of the youngest actresses to join the Forbes 30 Under 30 list of 2022.

Xochitl Gomez  is set to reprise her role as America Chavez in Avengers: Secret Wars (2027), an upcoming MCU crossover film.

She is also attached to star in Ursa Major (TBA), a sci-fi thriller film about a girl who discovers a bear-like creature in her backyard.

Xochitl Gomez Net Worth

Xochitl Gomez’s net worth is estimated to be around $2 million as of 2023.

She earns most of her income from her acting career, as well as from endorsements and sponsorships.

Xochitl Gomez  has worked with brands such as HotTopic clothing.

She is also active on social media platforms such as Instagram and TikTok, where she has over 400k followers and over 100k followers respectively.

Xochitl Gomez Spouse

Xochitl Gomez is not married and does not have a spouse.

She is also very private about her personal life and has not revealed any information about her dating history or current relationship status.

She is focused on her career and education at the moment.

However, she has expressed her support for the LGBTQ+ community and has played gay characters such as America Chavez and Dawn Schafer.

Conclusion

Xochitl Gomez is a rising star in Hollywood who has impressed audiences and critics alike with her versatile and charismatic performances in various movies and TV shows.

She is best known for playing the role of America Chavez, a superhero who can travel across dimensions, in the Marvel Cinematic Universe film Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness (2022).

Xochitl Gomez  is one of the youngest actresses to join the Forbes 30 Under 30 list of 2022 and has a bright future ahead of her.
