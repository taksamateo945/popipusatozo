Demon Slayer Watch Order for Arcs, Seasons and Movies

Publish date: 2024-03-28

Demon Slayer watch order traces how to chronologically watch Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba, a Japanese manga and anime series that gained widespread attention for its captivating story, well-developed characters, and stunning animation. It follows the journey of Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who was determined to become a demon slayer to avenge the death of his family and cure his sister Nezuko, who had turned into a demon.

There are several reasons why a Demon Slayer watch order is important for new and old fans of the series. First, the anime series is told in chronological order, which means that the events of each arc are presented in the order in which they happened in the manga. This is important because it helps you to understand the story and the characters. Some of the arcs in Demon Slayer are connected; hence, they share characters or events. Moreover, The Demon Slayer movies are canon, meaning they are part of the official story.

How To Watch Demon Slayer in Order

The full Arcs of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba include seasons and movies and a specific order in which one has to watch it to understand the whole story. Here’s a breakdown of how to watch Demon Slayer in order, including the TV series and movies.

TV Series – Season 1: Start by watching the first season of the Demon Slayer anime. The TV series covers the early arcs of the story. Here’s the order of episodes for Season 1:

Episodes 1-26: These episodes cover the main story arcs leading up to the Mugen Train arc.

Movie – Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba the Movie: Mugen Train: After completing Season 1, watch the first Demon Slayer movie, which adapts the Mugen Train arc. This movie is a continuation of the first season and is a significant part of the story.

TV Series – Continuation: After watching the Mugen Train movie, continue with the TV series for the events following the film. This portion of the TV series is also called the “Mugen Train Arc” and is sometimes labeled as Season 2.

Movie – “Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba the Movie: Entertainment District Arc” – This is the second Demon Slayer movie. It adapts the Entertainment District arc. It’s the next chronological part of the story after the Mugen Train arc. Be sure to watch this movie to continue the story.

TV Series – Season 3: It adapts the Swordsmith Village Arc and was released on April 9, 2023, and ran till June 18 with 12 episodes.

Demon Slayer Arcs in Order

In anime and manga, an “arc” refers to a narrative segment within a larger story that focuses on specific events, characters, or themes. It’s a way to structure the storytelling and progression of a series. Demon Slayer is divided into several story arcs, each with plot developments, character interactions, and challenges.

These arcs often revolve around the main character’s journey, battles against demons, character growth, and exploration of the series’ world and lore. Some arcs introduce new characters, expand on the backstory of existing characters, or provide resolutions to ongoing conflicts. Here is the order of the arcs in Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.

1. Final Selection Arc (Ep. 1-5): This arc introduces the protagonist, Tanjiro Kamado, as he undergoes the Final Selection to become a Demon Slayer. It’s a test of survival and combat skills for potential Demon Slayer recruits

2. Kidnapper’s Bog Arc (Ep. 6-7): After passing the Final Selection, Tanjiro receives his first mission as a Demon Slayer, leading him to confront a demon terrorizing a town.

3. Asakusa Arc (Ep. 8-10): Tanjiro, along with Nezuko and Zenitsu, investigates demons in the Asakusa area, leading to a confrontation with Lower Rank demons.

4. Tsuzumi Mansion Arc (Ep. 11-14): Tanjiro, Zenitsu, and Inosuke are sent to investigate disappearances in a mansion where they encounter Upper-Rank demons.

5. Natagumo Mountain Arc(Ep. 15-21): The trio faces a group of spiders that have turned into demons on Natagumo Mountain. This arc is a significant turning point in the series, showcasing the characters’ growth and challenges.

6. Rehabilitation Training Arc (Ep. 22-26): After the events on Natagumo Mountain, the main characters undergo training to improve their skills and abilities.

7. Mugen Train Arc (Ep. 27-34): This arc is covered in the “Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba the Movie: Mugen Train” film. Tanjiro, Nezuko, Zenitsu, and Inosuke board the Mugen Train to investigate disappearances and confront an Upper-Rank demon.

8. Entertainment District Arc (Ep. 34-44): This arc is set in the Yoshiwara Red Light District, where the Demon Slayer Corps investigates a string of disappearances linked to a powerful demon.

9. Swordsmith Village Arc (Ep. 45-55): Tanjiro and his friends visit the Swordsmith Village to repair their damaged swords and learn about the Breath of the Sun technique.

10. Hashira Training Arc (Manga): Tanjiro and the other Demon Slayers undergo training under the Hashira (Pillars) to become stronger in preparation for the battles ahead.

11. Infinity Castle Arc (Manga): This arc involves the Demon Slayer Corps’ assault on Muzan Kibutsuji’s headquarters to defeat him and end the demon threat.

12. Final Arc (Manga): The series concludes with the resolution of the conflict against Muzan Kibutsuji and the aftermath of the battle.

Demon Slayer Movies in Order

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – The Movie: Mugen Train: This animated movie was released in October 2020 in Japan. It adapts the Mugen Train Arc from the manga and directly continues the anime’s first season. It is the highest-grossing anime film of all time.

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – The Movie: To the Swordsmith Village (2023): This movie is a compilation film that includes footage from the final two episodes of Entertainment District Arc and an advanced screening of the first Swordsmith Village Arc episode. It is not yet available for streaming or DVD.

The Demon Slayer movies can be watched in any order, but watching the Mugen Train Arc before the Entertainment District Arc is recommended.

Demon Slayer Chronological Order

Demon Slayer anime is straightforward, as the series is presented in a linear narrative. The main anime adaptation covers the story chronologically without any significant deviations. Here’s the chronological watch order:

  • Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba (TV anime) [Episode 1-26]
  • Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Mugen Train Arc (Movie) [Episode 27-28]
  • Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Entertainment District Arc (TV anime) [Episode 29-present]

    • The Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba movie, Mugen Train Arc, is set after the events of the TV anime’s first season. However, it was released in theaters before the anime’s second season aired. So, if you want to watch the series chronologically, you should watch the movie after the anime’s first season.

    The Entertainment District Arc is the second season of the Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba anime. It is set after the events of the Mugen Train Arc. A few OVAs and specials have also been released for Demon Slayer. However, these are not essential to the main story, so you can watch them whenever you want.

    Demon Slayer Books in Order

    While there is a Demon Slayer watch order for anime, it is not needed for the manga, which is just as interesting as the anime. The manga is written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge, and it was serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump from 2016 to 2020. Even though the anime is faithful to the account of events in the manga, reading the books still has certain advantages. For example, the manga has more detail and backstory than the anime. Additionally, the manga is still ongoing, so you can read ahead to find out what happens next.

    1. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 1: Cruelty

    Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume One introduces readers to Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose life takes a tragic turn when his family is brutally slaughtered by demons, with only his sister Nezuko surviving, albeit turned into a demon herself. Determined to avenge his family and find a way to cure Nezuko, Tanjiro begins his journey as a demon slayer. He begins by undergoing training to better equip him for the task ahead.

    2. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 2: It Was You

    Volume two continues Tanjiro’s training as he hones his skills as a demon slayer. Alongside his allies, he faces a formidable threat in the form of a dangerous morphing demon. Tanjiro’s warrior growth and unwavering resolve to save his sister Nezuko remain at the forefront as the volume delivers intense battles and further explores the intricacies of the demon-slaying world.

    3. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 3: Believe in Yourself

    In volume three, Tanjiro faces off against the powerful and sadistic demon, “Mother Spider,” who controls a terrifying group of demon-spider hybrids. This volume delves into Tanjiro’s resourcefulness and determination as he confronts the demon’s treacherous traps and the relentless danger posed by the spider demons. As the battles intensify, Tanjiro’s growth as a demon slayer becomes more evident, showcasing his evolving combat skills and unwavering commitment to his mission.

    4. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 4: Robust Blade

    Volume four follows Tanjiro’s journey as he faces an unsettling demon who can manipulate dreams. Tanjiro’s determination is tested as he enters the demon’s dream realm, confronting his past and battling his inner fears. With his sister Nezuko by his side and his unwavering resolve, Tanjiro navigates the dream landscape in pursuit of victory.

    5. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 5: To Hell

    Volume five thrusts Tanjiro and his companions into the midst of an intense battle in a snowy mountain village plagued by demons. With his allies, including the boisterous Zenitsu and the self-assured Inosuke, Tanjiro fights against powerful demons in a fierce showdown. The volume explores each character’s unique strengths and challenges while highlighting their determination and growth. As the battles unfold in a chilling setting, Tanjiro’s resilience and camaraderie take center stage, promising an action-packed continuation of the Demon Slayer journey.

    6. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 6: The Demon Slayer Corps Gathers

    Volume six sees Tanjiro and his companions entering the mysterious Infinity Castle to confront the powerful Upper-Rank demon, Daki. They face a surreal and dangerous environment in the castle filled with illusions and traps. As Tanjiro’s group battles demons and navigates the treacherous castle, their determination and teamwork are put to the ultimate test. The volume delves into the character’s psychological and physical challenges while unraveling more about the demons’ world and hierarchy. With high-stakes battles and a complex setting, the story takes an intense turn, showcasing the heroes’ unwavering resolve against overwhelming odds.

    7. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 7: Trading Blows at Close Quarters

    Volume seven follows Tanjiro’s group as they continue their dangerous ascent through Infinity Castle, facing deadly traps and demons along the way. The tension and stakes escalate as they draw closer to the Upper-Rank demon. Tanjiro’s determination to rescue his sister and avenge his family’s death remains unshakable. The volume highlights the characters’ growth, showcasing their evolving abilities and the bonds that hold them together. With intricate battles and a relentless pursuit of their goal, this volume captures the intensity of their mission within the enigmatic castle’s depths.

    8. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 8: The Strength of the Hashira

    Volume eight delves into the backstory of the Upper-Rank demon, Hantengu, shedding light on his tragic past and the circumstances that led him to become a demon. As Tanjiro and his allies confront Hantengu’s multiple personalities and abilities, they face complex challenges that test their adaptability and teamwork.

    9. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 9: Operation: Entertainment District

    Volume nine takes Tanjiro and his companions on a new mission involving a demon connected to Tanjiro’s past. As they uncover the demon’s abilities and motives, they engage in a battle that pushes them to their limits. The volume delves into Tanjiro’s determination to protect and save even those who have turned into demons, showcasing his unwavering compassion. With intense action, character growth, and emotional resonance, this volume continues exploring the complexities of the demon slayer world and the characters’ choices.

    10. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 10: Human and Demon

    Volume ten follows Tanjiro and his allies as they confront a demon who has taken control of a train and its passengers. Boarding the Infinite Train, they face a unique and treacherous environment where the boundaries of reality blur.

    11. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 11: A Close Fight

    Volume eleven follows Tanjiro and his companions as they continue their perilous journey within the Infinite Train. They confront a powerful demon named Enmu, who manipulates dreams to trap his victims. As the battle unfolds in both the physical and dream realms, Tanjiro’s determination to protect his friends and overcome his own fears shines through.

    12. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 12: The Upper Ranks Gather

    Volume twelve continues Tanjiro and his group’s battle against the formidable demon Enmu on the Infinite Train. As they face Enmu’s cunning and his ability to exploit their deepest insecurities, their determination to prevail grows stronger.

    13. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 13: Transitions

    Volume thirteen delves into the past of Kyojuro Rengoku, one of the Hashira and a powerful flame Hashira. Tanjiro and his companions encounter Rengoku on the Flame Hashira’s mission to investigate a series of disappearances on a mountain.

    14. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 14: The Mu of Muichiro

    Volume fourteen continues the exploration of Kyojuro Rengoku’s story as Tanjiro learns more about the Flame Hashira’s background and motivations. Rengoku’s resolve is tested when he faces a powerful Upper-Rank demon on a moving train.

    15. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 15: Daybreak and First Light

    Volume fifteen follows the aftermath of the intense battles on the Infinite Train. Tanjiro and his group face the consequences of their encounters and mourn the loss of Kyojuro Rengoku. As they regroup and reflect, the volume delves into their emotional struggles and determination to continue their mission. With a focus on grief, resilience, and the bonds between characters, this volume provides a poignant pause in the midst of the demon-slaying action, highlighting the human side of their journeys.

    16. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 16: Indestructible

    Volume sixteen sees Tanjiro and his companions recovering from their losses and preparing for new challenges. They face a new mission involving a demon with the power to control people’s minds. As they confront the demon’s abilities and navigate the complexities of the situation, their determination to protect innocent lives remains strong.

    17. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 17: Successors

    Volume seventeen follows Tanjiro and his group as they confront a powerful demon that utilizes sound-based attacks. They face the challenges of battling a demon who exploits their weaknesses and disrupts their senses. The volume delves into the characters’ determination and resilience in the face of adversity, showcasing their strategic thinking. With intense sound-based battles and character development, this volume offers a blend of action and emotional depth while highlighting the heroes’ evolving combat skills within the world of demon slaying.

    18. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 18: Assaulted by Memories

    Volume Eighteen takes Tanjiro and his companions to an entertainment district to investigate a series of disappearances. They encounter a demon with the ability to manipulate emotions and create illusions. The volume explores the characters’ inner struggles and showcases their determination to save lives and unravel the mystery. With emotional battles and a focus on the power of human connection, this volume delves into the complexities of emotions and showcases the heroes’ unwavering commitment to their mission as they face a unique and challenging opponent.

    19. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 19: Flapping Butterfly Wings

    Volume nineteen continues Tanjiro and his companions’ mission to investigate the entertainment district. They confront a demon who has been terrorizing the area and manipulating the local populace. The volume delves into themes of identity and humanity, as well as the characters’ determination to protect the innocent. With action-packed battles and exploration of the demon-slaying world, this volume showcases Tanjiro’s growth and his allies’ camaraderie as they confront the challenges of their mission while uncovering the demons’ motives.

    20. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 20: The Path of Opening a Steadfast Heart

    Volume 20 sees Tanjiro and his companions confronting a demon who has taken over a children’s game to lure victims. As they navigate the dangerous game world, they must decipher its rules and challenges to save the children and defeat the demon. The volume explores the characters’ adaptability and resourcefulness while showcasing their determination to protect those in need.

    21. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 21: Ancient Memories

    Volume twenty-one continues Tanjiro and his group’s mission as they investigate a demon infestation in a village. They face a demon with the power to manipulate plants, creating a lush and dangerous environment. The volume delves into the characters’ resilience and determination to protect the village and its inhabitants.

    22. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 22: The Wheel of Fate

    In Volume twenty-two, Tanjiro and the Demon Slayer Corps face off against Muzan Kibutsuji, the king of demons. Tanjiro is severely injured, but he sees a vision of his ancestor that may hold the key to finally destroying Muzan.

    23. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – Volume 23: Life Shining Across the Years

    In Volume twenty-three, Tanjiro and the Demon Slayer Corps continue to fight Muzan Kibutsuji. Muzan unleashes his most powerful attacks, but Tanjiro and his allies are determined to defeat him. The battle reaches its climax as dawn approaches, and the fate of the world hangs in the balance.
