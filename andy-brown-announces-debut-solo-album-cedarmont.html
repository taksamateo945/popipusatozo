Andy Brown announces debut solo album 'Cedarmont'

Publish date: 2024-05-13

Andy Brown has announced that his debut solo album Cedarmont will be available to pre-order from tomorrow.

Taking to Twitter, Andy made the exciting announcement to his loyal fans. He also used Instagram to share the album’s artwork and tracklist. The album will include the previously heard ‘Landslide’, ‘Lay With Me’, ‘Breaking Your Heart’, and ‘Hollow’.

Cedarmont album tracklist

  • Talk of the Town
  • Lay With Me
  • Put That Record On
  • Hold On
  • Landslide (featuring Crissie Rhodes)
  • Keeper
  • About Last Night
  • Hollow
  • Breaking Your Heart
  • Honey
  • You Had Me At Hello
  • One Last Time

    • Andy will be releasing the next track from the album, ‘Talk of The Town’, at 8am tomorrow. The singer has been busy teasing the song on social media all week, and we can’t wait to hear what he has in store for us.

    2018 looks set to be an exciting year for Andy Brown. Not only will he be releasing his debut solo album, but he will also be heading on a UK tour in February. The singer will be visiting Birmingham, London, Manchester, Leeds and Glasgow.
