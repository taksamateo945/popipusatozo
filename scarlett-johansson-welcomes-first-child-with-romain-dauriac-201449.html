Scarlett Johansson Welcomes First Child With Romain Dauriac

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Scarlett Johansson is officially a mom! The Lucy star welcomed her first child, a baby girl named Rose, with fiance Romain Dauriac, her rep confirmed to the Associated Press on Thursday, Sept. 4.

The AP reports that "mother and daughter are doing well."

The 29-year-old actress kept a low profile prior to giving birth, last stepping out in New York City in July sporting a new mom-proof pixie hairdo.

In an interview with Vogue, the sex symbol gushed about her groom-to-be, telling the mag it's easier to be with a non-actor like her French journalist beau.
