Gwyneth Paltrow Gets Cozy in Bikini With Brad Falchuk in Italy: Photos

Publish date: 2024-02-17

Lather up! Gwyneth Paltrow is enjoying some much needed R&R with boyfriend Brad Falchuk. The Goop founder journeyed from Rome to Positano with her producer beau on Monday, July 13, and showcased her fit bod in an itty-bitty bikini.

Clad in white sunglasses and a two-piece string bikini, Chris Martin’s ex-wife, 42, got a hand with her sunscreen from Falchuk, 44. In between working on her bronze glow, the mom of two appeared to be having a blast diving off the cliffside into the gorgeous sea. 

“When in Rome … make your own pasta! Chef Rosa had me blend the egg with basil before I mixed with flour … Green tagliolini … Will post whole recipe soon on #goop,” Paltrow shared with followers on Instagram on Monday, along with a photo of raw pasta.

Us Weekly exclusively revealed the Iron Man star was dating the Glee co-creator in August 2014. The two first connected when Paltrow guest starred on the hit Fox series before turning things romantic. After getting flirty during a dinner date at East Borough in Los Angeles, the pair jetted off to Utah for a romantic weekend in July 2014. 

Since Paltrow and Martin consciously uncoupled in March 2014, the Coldplay frontman has been linked to Jennifer Lawrence.
