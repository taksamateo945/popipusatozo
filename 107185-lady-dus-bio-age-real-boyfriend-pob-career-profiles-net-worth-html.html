Career history

Duduzile's father taught her how to DJ at a young age, and she won her first DJ competition at 9. Lady Du worked as a somatologist and skin therapist in London to save money for her music career.

Lady Du worked on a ship that travelled the world. She was a beauty specialist, a teeth whitening professional, a safety officer and even studied fire fighting.

She quit her job in 2012 and returned to South Africa. She worked in her dad's shop and organized events for a foundation while deejaying but could not find a strong footing in SA's competitive music sector.

When her family pressured her to start a business or find a corporate job, Duduzile got a logistics manager job in 2016 but quit three years later because the situation took a toll on her mental health. The lady even attempted suicide in 2019 due to depression.

She got a breakthrough in 2020 when Mr JazziQ introduced her to Amapiano music. Lady Du's fame skyrocketed nationwide within a year, and she became a global superstar after a billboard in Times Square, New York City, displayed her picture.

Lady Du's Zuma Yi Star is one of her two latest hit tracks. She uploaded the official audio on YouTube on 10th July 2021. The track has over 1.8 million views as of August 2022.

Meanwhile, the official audio of Tchukutsha (Makhadzi ft Lady Du) was uploaded on 27th August 2021 and has over 200k views as of this writing.
