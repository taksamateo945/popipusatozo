Kendra Wilkinson Speaks Out on Sex Tape: I'm Not a &quot;Porn Star&quot;

Publish date: 2024-04-09

Kendra Wilkinson admits the release of her sex tape — reportedly shot when she was just 18 — has been hard on her husband of one year, Hank Baskett.

“It just sucks because it’s the hardest thing to deal with right now, and it’s hard on Hank,” she says in a preview clip from the May 31 finale of her E! show, Kendra. “It’s hard on him because it involves another guy, of course, and it’s hard on him because of our son and everything.”

Baskett, 27, is being supportive.

“He’s doing a good thing. He’s understanding. [He says], ‘We just have to hold our heads up high because we’re gonna get attacked, but we can’t run. We have to face it,'” says Wilkinson, 24. “It’s gonna be really hard. Probably the hardest time of our lives.”

Wilkinson says news that the tape would be released “broke my heart.”

“How could you do that when I have a baby?” implored Wilkinson, who welcomed Hank Baskett IV last December.
